01. Grind

Precision is key. A medium-fine grind ensures optimal extraction, balancing sweetness and acidity without bitterness.

02. Bloom

Saturate the grounds with twice their weight in water. Wait 30 seconds to release trapped gases for a cleaner cup.

03. Pour

Maintain a steady, slow spiral pour from the center outward. Control the flow to extract the full spectrum of flavors.
